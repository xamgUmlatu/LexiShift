LexiShift Language Pack URLs

Format:
pack_id: <stable id>
name: <display name>
type: monolingual | translation
languages: <source_langs> -> <target_langs> (use same for monolingual)
size: <exact size>
url: <download url>
wayback_url: <wayback wildcard url>
filename: <downloaded filename>
unzipped_name: <final filename or dir after extraction, if any>
layout: <brief structure description>
parse_hint: <delimiter/columns>
notes: <optional>
