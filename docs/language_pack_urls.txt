name: <display name>
type: monolingual | translation | embeddings
languages: <source_langs> -> <target_langs> (use same for monolingual)
size: <exact size>
url: <download url>
wayback_url: <wayback wildcard url>
filename: <downloaded filename>
unzipped_name: <final filename or dir after extraction, if any>
layout: <brief structure description>
parse_hint: <delimiter/columns>
notes: <optional>

-- Word Frequencies --

English Word Frequency (COCA 2026)
The Corpus of Contemporary American English (COCA) remains the industry standard. For 2026, the "Free 5,000" has been expanded in community mirrors to include more modern web-culture terms.
pack_id: freq-en-coca
name: COCA English Frequency List (Lemmatized)
size: 2 MB (CSV/TSV)
url: https://www.wordfrequency.info/samples/lemmas_60k.txt
backup_url: https://web.archive.org/web/20210127204059/https://www.wordfrequency.info/samples/lemmas_60k.txt
filename: en_freq_coca.csv
notes: Includes rank, lemma, pos, and frequency. This is the lemmatized version (e.g., "running" and "ran" are both under "run").

Japanese Word Frequency (BCCWJ)
The Balanced Corpus of Contemporary Written Japanese (BCCWJ) is the gold standard from NINJAL. Unlike most lists, it explicitly provides Short Unit Words (SUW) and Long Unit Words (LUW), which is vital for parsing Japanese compound nouns.
pack_id: freq-ja-bccwj
name: BCCWJ Japanese Lemma Frequency
size: 50 MB
url: https://repository.ninjal.ac.jp/record/3234/files/BCCWJ_frequencylist_suw_ver1_0.zip
backup_url: https://web.archive.org/web/0/https://repository.ninjal.ac.jp/record/3234/files/BCCWJ_frequencylist_suw_ver1_0.zip
filename: bccwj_suw_freq.zip
notes: Contains columns for Lemma, Etymology (Native/Sino-Japanese), and Freq. It uses UniDic-style lemmatization, which is the most accurate for NLP tasks in 2026.
